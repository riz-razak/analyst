Two rulers, one dispute

The Ministry’s ruler  Unaudited Two-year total

343 school days vs NIE’s 300

Counted over 10 Oct 2024 – 7 Aug 2026 — from the start of Grade 12. How 343 was reached has never been published: no working for cyclone days, Wednesday closures, or district variance.

The students’ question

“How much time to revise?”

The complaint is about the final run-in. A two-year total measures nothing about that — and NIE’s 300 is a teachers’ syllabus-completion guideline, not a student-revision standard.

The government’s only public defence answers a different question from the one being asked.

Two decisions, seven months apart
14 Dec 2025

Third-term exams cancelled nationwide; all students promoted —

“in consideration of children’s mental well-being and the time needed for normalcy to be restored after facing such a disaster.”

— Ministry Secretary Nalaka Kaluwewa. The 2026 cohort entered Grade 13 with no end-of-Grade-12 assessment.

Jul 2026

The A/L stays on 10 Aug 2026.

The same Ministry has declined to move the exam for the same cohort. The December reasoning has not been publicly addressed in the A/L decision.
