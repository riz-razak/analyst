# Counter-Propaganda Strand Map

This map breaks the response into strands that different teams can pick up.

## Strand 1: Source Genealogy

Goal: explain where `40,000` comes from.

Use:

- `sources.csv`
- `claims.csv`
- `casualty-estimate-map.csv`
- `un-report-inventory.csv`

Core message:

> Current evidence does not verify `40,000 deaths in Mullivaikkal alone`. The source trail points to a caveated final-phase Vanni casualty figure, not an exact or Mullivaikkal-only death toll.

## Strand 2: Number Discipline

Goal: stop death, missing, disappeared, unaccounted, and demographic-model categories being merged.

Use:

- `reference_numbers.csv`
- `casualty-estimate-map.csv`

Core message:

> Every number needs source, category, geography, time window, and method.

## Strand 3: Wikipedia And Mirrors

Goal: show how source language hardens through high-authority search surfaces.

Use:

- `wikipedia-revision-citation-audit.csv`
- `reference_claim_instances.csv`
- `reference_visual_nodes.csv`
- `reference_visual_edges.csv`

Core message:

> Wikipedia and mirrors can turn derivative wording into search infrastructure. Mirrors are not independent corroboration.

## Strand 4: Political Recognition

Goal: separate political recognition from legal or factual adjudication.

Use:

- `seo-top-100-capture.csv`
- `seo-power-distribution-summary.csv`

Core message:

> Political recognition can amplify a claim, but it does not by itself prove casualty numbers or legal determinations.

## Strand 5: Stakeholders And Incentives

Goal: map organizational capacity and incentives without overclaiming causation.

Use:

- `funding-and-incentive-nodes.csv`

Core message:

> Funding visibility and advocacy capacity are relevant context. They are not proof of paid message causation unless a money-to-message record exists.

## Strand 6: Viral Claim Audit

Goal: audit the viral Sinhala post claim by claim.

Use:

- `viral-post-claim-sheet.csv`

Core message:

> The post can be assessed sentence by sentence. The number claim is overstated as written if read as a UN-backed Mullivaikkal-only claim.

## Strand 7: Counter-Route Discipline

Goal: prevent state/pro-state correction from becoming denial.

Use:

- `casualty-estimate-map.csv`
- `sources.csv`
- `claims.csv`

Core message:

> Lower official and counter-narrative routes are necessary evidence, but they also require method and incentive review.
