Privacy Policy
analyst.rizrazak.com | Last Updated: February 2026
1. Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to analyst.rizrazak.com, an independent investigative journalism platform operated by Riz Razak. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages within United States jurisdiction.

2. Data We Collect

When you visit or interact with this site, we may collect:

  • Your IP address
  • User agent (browser and device information)
  • Timestamp of your visit and interaction
  • Text of any comments or submissions you provide
  • Approximate geographic location derived from IP
3. Why We Collect It

Data collection is conducted under Legitimate Interest as defined in GDPR Article 6(1)(f) for the following purposes:

  • Defence against defamation claims and legal liability
  • Identification of coordinated harassment or bad-faith campaigns
  • Prevention of abuse and spam
  • Maintaining the integrity of public discourse
4. How Long We Keep It
  • Raw IP addresses: Retained for 90 days, then irreversibly hashed and stored indefinitely for threat detection.
  • User agent and timestamp: Retained indefinitely for audit purposes.
  • Comment text: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion.
  • Geolocation data: Derived on-the-fly from IP; not stored separately.
5. Who Sees It

Data is accessible only to the site operator (Riz Razak). It will be disclosed to external parties only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • To defend against legal claims
  • With your explicit consent

Data will never be sold, traded, or used for marketing.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal hold requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, email riz@dgtl.lk with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Storage

We do not use cookies.

We use browser localStorage only to store your consent preferences (GDPR banner dismissal). localStorage is never used for tracking or profiling.

8. International Data Transfers

This site is hosted in the United States. By visiting this site, you consent to the transfer of your data to the United States where it is subject to U.S. law.

9. GDPR Compliance

Although this site is hosted in the United States, we honor GDPR rights as a courtesy to EU visitors. All GDPR rights mentioned above apply equally to all users regardless of location.

Our Legal Basis for processing under GDPR is Article 6(1)(f) (Legitimate Interest).

10. Contact

For privacy inquiries, complaints, or to exercise your rights, contact:

Email: riz@dgtl.lk
