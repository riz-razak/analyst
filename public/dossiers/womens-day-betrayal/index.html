The Women's Day Betrayal:
How a State Newspaper Platformed an Alleged Sexual Predator to Celebrate Feminism

කාන්තා දිනයේ විශ්වාසභංගය:
රාජ්‍ය පුවත්පතක් ස්ත්‍රීවාදය සැමරීමට ලිංගික අපචාරකරුවෙකු වේදිකාගත කළ ආකාරය

On International Women's Day 2026, the Sunday Observer's Observer MUSE supplement published a feature celebrating Simone de Beauvoir's feminist philosophy — and chose a man with documented sexual harassment allegations as a "key resource person." Multiple women have publicly posted screenshot evidence of his uninvited sexual messages. This is that story.
