## What the live audit changes / adds

1. **The right comparable for a NEWS volume engine is Ada Derana's churn**, not Hiru. Hiru's subscriber
   lead is **teledrama-driven** — an entertainment lever The Analyst will not use. Our volume benchmark
   = Ada Derana's news cadence (clips + reels + bulletins + 24/7 live), executed *with context*.
2. **Both run owned, multi-surface distribution** — Hiru explicitly lists Facebook, Instagram, TikTok,
   X, Threads, a **WhatsApp channel**, and Android/iOS **apps**. This validates the Philosophy Council
   guardrail: **own the distribution spine** (site/email/WhatsApp/app), use rented feeds as on-ramps.
3. **Format stack to copy** (seen live on Ada Derana): 35–90s clip + headline reel + full bulletin +
   24/7 live, all from one capture — the repurpose-once-distribute-many engine.

## Adopt / reject (unchanged, now live-grounded)

- **Adopt:** Ada Derana's news cadence + format stack + owned WhatsApp/app surfaces.
- **Reject:** decontextualised churn (Ada Derana averages low engagement per item — volume without
  retention) and any communal bait. Our differentiator stays: **volume WITH context** — every clip
  carries source + "what it means", trading their spray-and-pray for credibility that compounds.
