# The Analyst → Media Company — Phase-1 Build Plan

"Baby step, but the baby must be stable." Scale The Analyst from a solo brand into a full media company,
widening from Sri Lanka to the **pan-South-Asian diaspora**, and reach **~$600/mo fast**. Synthesis of
the full council sequence the founder convened: Hiru/Derana research (3 rounds), Philosophy Council
(Atsuko lead, 3 rounds), business planning (5 rounds, 2 adversarial), Hasib finance (2 rounds).

## 1. Identity — who we are as a media company (Philosophy Council)

> **News with context, at scale, for the rooted majority — volume as an instrument of understanding,
> never of outrage.** Where Hiru and Derana use volume to manufacture outrage and sell agitated
> audiences to power, we use volume to manufacture *understanding*: every piece, however fast or short,
> carries the context that turns a scandal into a visible system. Diaspora and regional reach **fund and
> amplify** the mission but never **govern or re-center** it — the Sri Lankan rooted base remains who we
> serve and answer to. We are not Hiru with better grammar; we are proof that volume can be liberation,
> not extraction.

## 2. The strategic unlock (why this breaks the bottleneck)

The traffic-to-revenue ceiling was Sri Lanka's low ad value. Two moves break it:

- **Pan-South-Asian diaspora (~44M; India 18M, Bangladesh 7.4M, Pakistan 6.3M; UAE 6.2M, US 6.1M, UK 4M,
  Canada 3M).** The same *extractive-system* frame (debt traps, dynasties, crony capitalism, press
  freedom, Gulf labour) is recognised across all of South Asia — "we're one machine." YouTube and ads
  **pay by viewer location**: English content to US/UK diaspora earns **~$8 RPM vs ~$0.70 domestic** — a
  6–10× multiplier. *(Identity guardrail: widen the **frame** and the **funding**, not the rooted mission.)*
- **A volume engine** (Hiru/Derana cadence) weaponised for context.

## 3. Hiru/Derana teardown — adopt the engine, reject the toxicity (MERIDIAN/Nuwan)

Scale: **Ada Derana** ~3.8M subs, **176k videos** (~40–50/day churn, 38 avg likes) — coverage machine.
**Hiru** ~5.1M subs, 59k videos, **648 avg likes** — higher hit-rate. (Aggregator estimates; Chrome was
not connected — a live channel audit is a recommended follow-up.)

- **Adopt:** high cadence; the *one capture → clip + short + full + 24/7 live* format stack;
  repurpose-once-distribute-many; speed on breaking events; language/regional + diaspora splits;
  volume-as-discovery.
- **Reject:** decontextualised outrage; communal/ethnic/religious bait (the load-bearing risk — the
  SWRD parallel); churnalism; deceptive thumbnails; engagement-at-any-cost.
- **"Volume with context" (operating rule):** no post ships without (a) what happened, (b) the source,
  (c) one line of *what it means*. Compete on speed of *credible* clips, never raw ones.

## 4. Content pipelines (Ridma)

- **A — Statement pieces (Vice-style, ~1/mo):** the brand and worldview; citation magnets for Segment E.
- **B — Big stories (2–4/mo):** investigative news-with-context; the lawyered substance.
- **C — Shorts (5+/wk → 1–3/day):** the acquisition + virality engine — hook + one structural insight +
  "full context at the link." **Firewall:** every Short's factual claim must trace to a published,
  sourced dossier (keeps legal exposure on the slow, lawyered pipeline). Shorts are *reach*, not revenue.

## 5. Regional investigator network (Atsuko/Sahani)

A new breed of **agile local investigators** feeding "news with context," paid **per accepted piece**
(cooperative fair-pay = recruiting edge vs extractive media). **Phase 1: 3–5 contributors** (not 10) —
e.g. SL base, Gulf kafala/labour, UK/Canada diaspora, one Shorts creator. Grow to **10 → 50** only as
revenue and **verification capacity** keep pace. Positional strength: not faster than Derana's breaking
tweet, but first to the *explanation* 6–24h later — a defensible, ownable window.

> New-country coverage in Phase 1 = **sourced analysis of public reporting** (clearly framed, citing
> originals) + low-risk diaspora-life original reporting. Original investigation in new jurisdictions
> waits until verification capacity exists (Mara).

## 6. Channel ladder → TV (Senevi)

Socials/Shorts → YouTube long-form → YouTube toward 24/7 → **Traditional TV**. TV is the *output of a
proven engine*, not the thing you build to get one. **TV trigger conditions (all must be true):** digital
revenue exceeds digital cost; a repeatable, licensable show format exists; a broadcast-capable legal
entity exists; the engine runs without the founder in every loop. → **TV is a Phase 3+ horizon; a
someday-trigger, not a Phase-1 task.**

## 7. Finance — the path to ~$600/mo, cost & scaling (Hasib, 2 rounds)

**Round 1 — fastest credible path:** **membership is the backbone.** $600 = **120 members @ $5** or 60 @
$10. A realistic stack (current model inputs): ~80 members ($400) + YouTube long-form ($120) + diaspora
display ($210) + Shorts/tips ($65) ≈ **$795 — clears $600 with headroom.** Shorts (RPM ~$0.10) are
acquisition; **traffic fills the membership funnel — trust converted to membership is the revenue.**

**Round 2 — cost & scaling:** Phase-1 burn (3–5 paid-per-piece contributors + tools + infra) ≈ **$546/mo**;
against the revenue stack, Phase 1 nets **~+$250/mo** — a bootstrap loop (members fund the contributors
who make the content that wins the members). Scaling cost is **variable** (paid per accepted piece, no
idle salary); central overhead per contributor falls with scale (4→10→50 contributors: total cost
~$880 → $2,000 → $8,500/mo; members-to-cover ~176 → 400 → 1,700). **Binding ceiling = verification
capacity, not writer count.** Model: `analyst-media-company-finance-v0.4.xlsx`. *(Tax: Tier-1 salary is
~tax-free at the LKR 1.8M relief — see v0.3; verify with Vinita/IRD.)*

## 8. Guardrails (Philosophy Council — build these BEFORE opening the throttle)

1. **No-context-no-publish** — context is the irreducible per-post unit.
2. **Compliance labels + two-source rule enforced per item, including AI output** (the verification spine).
3. **Communal-bait structural ban** — never frame extraction as ethnic/religious villainy (load-bearing wall).
4. **Cultivate comprehension, not outrage** — KPIs = deep-read / source-click / cross-dossier nav, not raw reach.
5. **Revenue–editorial firewall** — diaspora/advertiser money funds; the rooted base governs.
6. **Mass-base audience floor** — SL + Sinhala-engaged serving must not fall below a set ratio as diaspora revenue grows.
7. **Own the distribution spine** (site/email/WhatsApp = home; social = on-ramp). **Surplus flows back** to the base.

## 9. Phased roadmap (Senevi)

**Phase 1.0 — stabilise the baby (wk 1–4):** submit Google Search Console (still undone); restructure
homepage into a **South Asia desk** (SL/India/Pakistan/Bangladesh/Diaspora/Geopolitics); stand up
**"Support the Desk" membership** + tip jar; launch the **Shorts pipeline from the existing ~5 dossiers**
(30+ Shorts backlog, zero new reporting); publish **one statement piece** ("South Asia is one machine").

**Phase 1.1 — feed the funnel (wk 5–10):** Shorts to 5+/wk, regionally voiced; diaspora-life + geopolitics
angles; recruit 3–5 paid-per-piece contributors; community-native distribution into specific
WhatsApp/Reddit/FB rooms.

**Phase 1.2 — prove the model (wk 11–16):** hit ~$600/mo on memberships + tips; one channel >10k
followers; begin YouTube long-form (toward monetisation); formalise verification capacity.

**Phase 1.5+ (post-$600):** network 5→10→50 as revenue covers it; YouTube toward 24/7; TV only when its
four triggers are true.

## 10. Mara's hardening conditions (non-negotiable)

Cadence (not virality) is the metric · firewall the pipelines (Shorts only point at defended claims) ·
no original investigation in new countries until verification capacity exists · 3–5 contributors in
Phase 1, not 10 · don't bet $600 on ads (members are the backbone) · TV is a trigger not a task ·
sequencing is survival (ship 1.0 fully before 1.1).
