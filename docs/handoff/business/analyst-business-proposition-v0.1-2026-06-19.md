# The Analyst — Business Proposition v0.1

**Question (founder, this session):** Can ONE clean ad per page sustainably fund a **Tier-1 analyst
salary** — LKR 150,000 ≈ **US$500/mo** (Yan tiers: 150k/300k/500k ≈ $500/$1,000/$1,500)? Working
backward from $500: how many ads, how much traffic, what workload, where's breakeven, what scenarios?

> **Council & method.** Run under the Yan RDS protocol via the `finance` committee (Hasib lead, Harini,
> Priya), with `adversarial` (Mara) and the Philosophy Forum. **Round 1** ran as real council nodes
> (outputs below); Rounds 2–5 are synthesised here. The methodology you specified — progressive,
> exponentially-widening research nodes per round + adversarial + financial synthesis — is rendered at a
> tractable scale; §7 lists how to fan out the deeper rounds. Per SD-COMMS-COMPLIANCE-2, every number
> traces to a cited source (workbook `Sources` tab).

---

## 1. Headline verdict — [KILLED as stated], [ENDORSED as hybrid]

**One ad per page cannot fund $500/mo in Year 1.** To net $500 (grossed ~15% for fees/forex/fill/
ad-block ⇒ **$594/mo gross**), a single clean ad needs **~300,000–475,000 pageviews/month** at realistic
blended RPM. A new Sri Lankan investigative site runs **5k–50k pv/mo** in year one — a **3×–19× gap**.

**What works instead:** **membership-led + one clean ad as a thin supplement.** At Base traffic (~60k
pv/mo) the ad yields ~$90; ~**168 members at $3/mo** closes the rest. That is on-ethos (community-funded)
and ad-independent. Ad-*alone* becomes viable only at **~250k–300k pv/mo** (Year 2–3, diaspora-tilted,
or a multi-analyst stable).

So: **yes, keep one clean ad per page — but as garnish, never the meal.** The salary must be funded
primarily by the people the platform serves.

## 2. Round 2 — Financial synthesis (Hasib #30)

Backward math (workbook `Backward Math` + `Scenarios`):

- **Gross needed:** ($500 salary + $16 infra) × 1.15 gross-up = **$594/mo**.
- **One clean ad RPM (effective):** Sri Lanka **$0.50** (AdSense tier T9, the lowest; news single unit
  $0.30–1.00), diaspora/Tier-1 **$3.00** (EthicalAds ~$2.50 clean contextual on EU/NA; Tier-1 news
  single unit $2–5). Blended by traffic mix.

| Scenario (mo-12) | Pageviews | SL/diaspora | Blended RPM | Ad rev/mo | Pageviews needed (ad-only) | Gap | Members to close gap |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lean | 25,000 | 70/30 | $1.25 | $31 | ~475,000 | **19.0×** | 188 |
| Base | 60,000 | 60/40 | $1.50 | $90 | ~396,000 | **6.6×** | 168 |
| Growth | 130,000 | 55/45 | $1.63 | $211 | ~365,000 | **2.8×** | 128 |
| Diaspora-tilt | 100,000 | 40/60 | $2.00 | $200 | ~297,000 | **3.0×** | 132 |

**How many ads?** One clean ad/page is the right call (more ads barely move low-RPM revenue and damage
trust). **How much traffic for ad-only?** ~300k pv/mo at best (diaspora-heavy), ~475k pure-SL —
unreachable in year one. **Breakeven (realistic, hybrid):** ad + **~130–190 recurring members** at any
scenario; or membership alone ≈ **~200 members at $3** covers salary+infra with zero ad dependence.

## 3. Round 3 — Adversarial (Mara #14)

Ranked failure modes for "one ad funds the salary":
1. **Traffic gap (fatal):** needs ~0.3–1.6M pv/mo; new SL site has tens of thousands. A missing zero.
2. **Ad-block erasure:** your engaged 18–34 cohort blocks ads at the highest rates — the more "Segment A/D" you are, the closer single-unit RPM trends to zero.
3. **AdSense single-point/ban risk:** one "invalid traffic" flag or sensitive-political-content strike (your own threat model names betting syndicates who could fake-click) = permanent ban, one appeal.
4. **Ethos contradiction:** renting reader attention to an ad monopoly is the extraction the platform indicts.
5. **Sovereignty surrender:** you host offshore to escape SL coercion, then hand the revenue lifeline to Google — a foreign chokepoint with no recourse (contradicts Yan independence directives).
6. **Solo key-person collapse:** the funding model and the human are the same single point of failure.

**What would have to be true** to survive: diaspora becomes the *majority* of traffic; salary is a
*lagging reward of traction*, not a founding promise; revenue is diversified (ad never load-bearing);
a bus-factor/content-buffer exists; and either strategy.md is amended or the "ad" is house-promotion of
the cooperatives.

## 4. Round 4 — Ethos (Philosophy Forum: Ajitha #24, Vishwa #28, Lumen #29)

- **[CONTESTED → false-equivalence rejected]:** a clean, server-side, context-only ad (no tracking, no
  RTB) is genuinely *different* from surveillance advertising. The clean/dirty distinction is real.
- **[KILLED]:** but selling a **third-party** ad slot to fund a *survival-critical* salary fails on the
  **independence axis** (Pillar 2). In Sri Lanka the advertiser pool overlaps the investigation pool
  (politicians, betting, finance, contractors); a load-bearing advertiser is a **patron with editorial
  veto** — reimporting the *wedana*/patronage dynamic the platform exists to dissolve.
- **[ENDORSED]:** a **house slot carrying the platform's own cooperative ventures** (cross-subsidy, not
  extraction) — prefiguratively positive.
- **If ads are pursued anyway**, six joint conditions: no behavioural tracking; sector-exclusion list;
  ≤20% of revenue from all ads; full advertiser transparency; written editorial firewall; commons-first.
  *The forum's finding: those conditions shrink ad revenue to near-zero — which is the tell.*
- **On-ethos revenue, ranked:** (1) **membership/subscription** (the only D156-sanctioned extraction),
  (2) **cooperative cross-subsidy** (the intended Pillar-2 model), (3) **capped diaspora patronage**
  (many small recurring gifts, per-donor cap), (4) **aligned no-strings grants** (bridge only),
  (5) tip-jar / paid deep-dossier tier.

> **Governance flag:** if the founders want ads as a real revenue line, `strategy.md` Pillar 2 ("no
> corporate sponsorship… not funded by anyone with an agenda") must be **formally amended** — otherwise
> the funding model is a documented self-contradiction and itself becomes a credibility attack vector.

## 5. Round 5 — Workload & scaling (Atsuko #20, Ridma #44, Sahani #35)

- **"1 big + 1 small per working day" is 3–5× over sustainable solo capacity.** A defensible dossier
  (two-source rule, Alleged Checker, compliance labels, court-defensible) is a **3–10 day** unit.
  Realistic solo output: **~2–4 big + 8–12 small pieces/month.**
- The analyst is four jobs in one body (researcher + journalist + marketer + brand). The **breaking
  point** is the collision of the publish spike and the marketing spike on launch day — under volume
  pressure the invisible **verification** step gets cut first, which is existential (credibility is the
  #1 asset; SL legal threat environment is unforgiving).
- **Traffic is share-led (WhatsApp + diaspora FB) and power-law:** ~2–4 hit pieces drive 60–80% of
  yearly traffic. Model pageviews as **lumpy midpoints, not floors**.
- **Scaling = a stable of branded analysts**, each a named public person with a beat, sharing
  **centralized legal/verification/SEO/distribution**. The binding constraint on scaling is
  **verification/legal capacity, not article volume.**

## 6. Recommendation (Priya #9 synthesis)

1. **Do not promise the Tier-1 salary off ads.** Fund it: **membership (primary) + cooperative
   cross-subsidy (Phase 2) + one clean, non-tracking, sector-screened ad as supplement (≤20%).**
2. **Treat the salary as a lagging reward of traction** — trigger it at ≈**200 members** or ≈**250k
   pv/mo**, not as a day-one assumption (respects YAN.md anti-hallucination + founder-currently-jobless).
3. **Set sustainable output:** ~3 big + ~10 small/month solo; protect verification absolutely.
4. **First reinvestment is de-collision** (a shared social/Sinhala packager + part-time legal), not more
   articles — then replicate the branded-analyst unit.
5. **Decision needed:** keep `strategy.md` no-ads ethos and go membership-only, **or** amend Pillar 2 to
   permit the conditioned clean ad. The council recommends membership-led either way.
